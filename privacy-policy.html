Privacy Policy

Family Stickers — Last updated: March 23, 2026

Family Stickers does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All information you enter in the app (family member names, ages, and settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any third-party SDKs.

Camera Roll

If you choose to add a background photo or save your wallpaper, the app requests access to your photo library. Photos are used only within the app and are never transmitted anywhere.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at tildenappstudio@miningdata.org.
